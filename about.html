Bio

My time at the Harvard Extension Coding Bootcamp has not been easy. Working forty two hours a week, fifty over the holiday season, and driving from Quincy has made be quite tired over the past six months. But in the end, I know my hard work and determination will pay off. Since being at the bootcamp I have become proficient in CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, MySQL, and MongoDB. When I'm not slouched over at my desk coding i enjoy standing at my desk practicing my turntables, hiking, camping, baseball, and riding my bike around Wampatuck State Park.
